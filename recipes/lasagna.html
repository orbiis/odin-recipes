Lasagna

Delecious vegan lasagna

Made with tofu

and spinach

Ingredients

    lasagna sheets
    tofu
    spinach
    leeks
    garlic
    Oat single cream

Steps

    Cut leeks and garlic
    Saute all the ingredients together
    "Pre-coock" the shits by dipping them in boiling water
    Layer everything together
    Cover in cooking cream
    Bake for 30 mins
