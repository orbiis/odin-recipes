Lasagna 2

Delicious vegan lasagna 2

Made with tofu

and spinach


Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tofu
  • Spinach
  • Leeks
  • Garlic
  • Oat single cream

Steps

  1. Cut leeks and garlic
  2. Sautee the ingredients together
  3. "Pre-coock" the sheets by dipping them in boiling water
  4. Layer everything together
  5. Cover in cooking cream
  6. Bake for 30 mins
